About Additive Artisan

Our Story

Welcome to Additive Artisan, where cute meets craft! I'm Rob, and I create cutsie 3D printed products with a personal touch.

Every print is crafted with care, one layer at a time. Whether you're looking for unique MTG accessories, fun gaming props, or custom creations, each piece is made with attention to detail and a whole lot of heart.

Robert Sears

Meet Artie - Your Craft Companion

Artie is our beloved mascot - an all-black craft-maker cat inspired by the charm of 1950s Hanna-Barbera cartoons. With his blue denim apron, amber stitching, and trusty pocket protector full of pens and pencils, he embodies the spirit of handmade creativity.
